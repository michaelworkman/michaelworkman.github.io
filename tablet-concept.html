Boston Globe tablet concept

As part of a hack-day project, I created a wireframe for a Boston Globe sports app. The app would serve as a season chronology for each of the four major professional sports teams in Boston. I wanted the app to be the place where fans turn to answer the question: “Who won the game last night?”


The heart of the app is today’s game in the user’s chosen sport. The screen has all of the relevant info about the most recent game, along with Globe coverage and analysis of the game. From this screen, the user can swipe right to see scheduled games, and swipe left to see previous games. Up or down swipe switches sports. Each screen has a graph showing the team’s progress through the four seasons.”


Other features I imagined were giving the user the ability to set wakeup alarms with different tones based on the result of the game, as well as to receive alerts when Globe stories are posted.”
