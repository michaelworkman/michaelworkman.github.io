I am an award-winning designer, a leader who excels at mentoring, and a strategic thinker who has helped shape new sites like BetaBoston.com, Cruxnow.com and BDCwire.com.


As design director of BostonGlobe.com, I lead a team of designers and developers who work on projects ranging from interactive data visualization to customized editorial presentations to UX and visual design for the Globe platform.


I am also fortunate to have the chance to keep my hand in design through editorial projects like Chasing Bayla, as well as through the redesign of BetaBoston.com, the Globe’s tech vertical.


I’ve always relished telling great stories through design. My recent work has focused on discovering user needs and designing great experiences to meet those needs.
