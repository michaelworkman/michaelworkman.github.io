About me

I’m an avid traveler who collects miles and points to take trips I’d never be able to afford at full price. I enjoy meeting new people and learning firsthand about other cultures. I am always planning the next trip!

I love movies of all kinds, but especially the classics. Spending time in a dark theater under a bright screen always inspires me.

I like drinking wine with friends over a good meal, and learning about it as an expression of the place and time it was created.

My favorite form of exercise is swimming, and I have completed mile swims in the Charles River and the Boston Harbor.
