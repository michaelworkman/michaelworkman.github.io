Chasing Bayla

Chasing Bayla is the story of one man’s quest to save the right whale from extinction.


We knew we had powerful photos and videos that illustrated the plight of Bayla, a whale that Michael Moore worked to save. Instead of having the visuals interspersed throughout the piece, we decided to provide a clean reading experience. But we also wanted to give the user the option of experiencing the story entirely in visuals, or to switch back and forth while reading the story.


I worked with developer Russell Goldenberg to create a design that allows the user to easily toggle between the two “sides” of the story — text and visuals.


With all of the great photos and videos we had with the story, we felt that illustrations would bring an added emotional dimension. I asked illustrator Matthew Woodson to create layered illustrations that would allow for subtle animations using CSS.
